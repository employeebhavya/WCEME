Submission Guidelines Details

1. General Instructions

  • Abstracts must be submitted online through the designated email.
  • Submissions must be made before the deadline: 15th September 2025.
  • Only original, unpublished work will be considered.

2. Abstract Format

  • Title: Bold, centered, not exceeding 20 words.
  • Authors: Full names with affiliations and contact information (mark the presenting author with an asterisk *).
  • Font: Times New Roman, size 12, single-spaced.
  • Length: Maximum 250–300 words (excluding title and author info).
  • Margins: 1 inch on all sides.
  • File format: .doc / .docx / PDF.

3. Content Structure

A structured abstract is preferred and must include:

  • Background: Brief context or rationale for the study.
  • Objectives: Aim or hypothesis of the work.
  • Methods: Description of methods, design, or tools used.
  • Results: Key findings
  • Conclusion: Implications
  • Keywords: 3–5 keywords (in italics, at the end).

4. Categories / Themes

Indicate the category/theme under which your abstract should be reviewed (e.g., Pediatrics, Public Health, Simulation Education, etc.).

5. Review Process

  • All abstracts will be peer-reviewed by the Scientific Committee.
  • Accepted abstracts will be eligible for oral or poster presentation.
  • Notification of acceptance will be sent by 18th September 2025

6. Submission Limit

Each author may submit up to 2 abstracts (1 for oral & 1 for poster) as presenting author.

7. Ethical Considerations

For studies involving human/animal subjects, ensure ethical clearance and mention it in the abstract if applicable.

Ready to Submit Your Abstract?

Please ensure your abstract follows all the guidelines above before submitting. You can submit up to 2 abstracts (1 for oral & 1 for poster presentation).
