About / The Conference

The World Congress of Emergency Medicine Educators (WCEME) is a premier international forum dedicated to the advancement of Emergency Medicine. Bringing together leading clinicians, academic faculty, experts, and healthcare administrators from across the globe, this conference aims to engage in cutting-edge discussions, hands-on workshops, and breakthrough research shaping the future of emergency care.

This conference is organised by the Department of Emergency Medicine, Aarupadai Veedu Medical College, Vinayaka Mission’s Puducherry Campus in collaboration with the Association of Emergency Medicine Educators. The theme is “Learning by Doing: Advancing Hands-On Training in Emergency Medicine”.
