Making an Effective Poster

Follow these three essential steps to create a compelling research poster

1

Plan the Poster

A poster is not a "manuscript on the wall." Storyboard your concept effectively.

  • Create a rough draft layout
  • Use bullet points, not heavy text
  • Include images, graphs, tables
  • Present most important findings only

Sections

Introduction, Objectives, Methods, Results, Conclusions, References (optional), Acknowledgements (optional)

2

Use PowerPoint

Create your poster using ONE PowerPoint slide with strategic design elements.

  • Sans-serif fonts (Arial, Calibri)
  • High contrast backgrounds
  • Use View → Gridlines for alignment
  • Add borders to images
  • Include AEME & institutional logos
3

Review & Print

Thorough review ensures professional quality and error-free presentation.

  • Check grammar and spelling
  • Ensure readable font sizes
  • Maintain 30% empty space
  • Review at actual poster size
  • Consider paper quality for travel

Final Check

Spacing and alignment often change when printed from different computers. Always check proofs carefully before final printing.
